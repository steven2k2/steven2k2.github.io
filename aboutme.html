About Me

I am a Front-End & Mobile App Developer with over 20 years of experience creating responsive web applications for enterprise environments. My focus is on building elegant, functional applications that seamlessly meet client needs.

  • Dedicated to delivering user-centred solutions that exceed expectations.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about exploring mid-century design, working on home automation projects, and staying up to date with NASA’s latest advancements. These hobbies fuel my creativity and problem-solving mindset.
